Some words about donations for my work

I don't have a donation address, but If you like to donate for my work, please directly donate to any Rom Project to keep our beloved roms alive.

Another way to make me happy would be if you donate to an organization who cares about (kids having) cancer. I had blood cancer in 2001 (when i was 12/13 years old) and i am happy being still alive.

A good choice of an organisation to donate to would be the DKMS (they also accept donations via paypal), but any other similar organisation will be good too.
